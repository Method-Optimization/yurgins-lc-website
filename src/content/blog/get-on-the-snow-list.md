Every winter it happens: the forecast turns, the first real storm rolls in, and suddenly everyone is scrambling for someone to clear their driveway. By then, the good crews are already booked solid with the customers who planned ahead.

That's why our residential snow removal is **enrollment-based** — and why the list fills before the first flake falls.

## How our snow service works

- **December through March**, for enrolled customers only. Enrolling locks in your spot on the route.
- We clear **driveways, walkways, and the access areas** you actually need to get in and out.
- Work happens **after the storm ends** — every enrolled property is cleared **within 24 hours** of snowfall stopping.
- **Salt and ice-melt** application is available as an add-on for extra traction on slick spots.

## Why enroll early?

Two reasons. First, **capacity**: a route can only hold so many homes, and once it's full, it's full. Second, **peace of mind**: when a storm is coming, enrolled customers don't have to do anything. You're already on the list, and we already have your property.

## Who it's for

If you'd rather not be out there with a shovel at 6 a.m. — or you've got a parent, a steep driveway, or an early commute to think about — a reliable snow plan is worth its weight. No scrambling, no last-minute calls, no waiting days to get plowed out.

Spots are limited and they go early. If winter snow is on your mind, get on the list now — not when the first storm is already in the forecast.
